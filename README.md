## 🚀 What I’m Building

### 🔥 ConceptPulse — AI-Powered Precision Learning Platform  
**Founder • Actively Building • Long-Term Mission**

ConceptPulse is a **precision learning engine** designed to fix the root problem in Indian education: **blind studying**.

Most students study hard but fail because they don’t know:
- what they’re weak at  
- why concepts don’t stick  
- whether they’ll remember after a week  

**ConceptPulse focuses on diagnosis, correction, and retention — not content overload.**

**Core capabilities:**
- 🔍 AI-driven diagnosis of concept & prerequisite gaps (under 30 seconds)
- 🧠 5–7 minute targeted corrections (no long videos)
- 🔁 Enforced spaced repetition (Day 1, 3, 7, 15, 30)
- 📊 Behavioral tracking (focus drops, avoidance, consistency breakdown)
- 👨‍👩‍👦 Transparent dashboards for parents & teachers

> ConceptPulse moves education from **reactive coaching** to **preventive learning**.
